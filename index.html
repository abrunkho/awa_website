A Framework for Exploration

Created by scientists, educators, and technologists, Are We Alone? is a web-based framework of experiences for anyone interested in learning more about the scientific search for life.


Are We Alone? blends virtual exploration of Mars, Europa, and beyond with adaptive, personalized learning experiences that engage learners in the process of discovery.


It’s more than a single experience. Are We Alone? is a framework that expands with new discoveries, driven by a growing network of scientists and educators.
